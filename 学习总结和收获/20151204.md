### node.insertBefore(newNode,node)   可以在当前节点的前面插入新的元素
```
<div class="parent">
    <div class="sub"></div>
</div>
sub.parentNode.insertBefore(newNode,sub);

//output:
<div class="parent">
    <div class="newNode"></div>
    <div class="sub"></div>
</div>
```
